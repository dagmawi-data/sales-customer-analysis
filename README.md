# sales-customer-analysis
Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis
Objective

To analyze sales data and uncover key insights about product performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends to support business decision-making.

Dataset
Source: Kaggle Superstore Dataset
Records: ~9,800 rows
Features: Customer, Product, Sales, Region, Dates
Key Business Questions
1. Which product categories generate the most revenue?
Technology products lead in total sales, indicating high demand and profitability.
2. Which products perform best?
Sub-categories like Phones and Chairs dominate sales, suggesting focus areas for inventory and marketing.
3. Who are the most valuable customers?
A small percentage of customers contribute significantly to revenue, highlighting the importance of customer retention strategies.
4. When do sales peak?
Sales show seasonal trends, with specific months generating higher revenue, useful for planning promotions and stock.
5. Delivery Efficiency
Delivery times vary across regions, indicating potential areas for logistics improvement.
